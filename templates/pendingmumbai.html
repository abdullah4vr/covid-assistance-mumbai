How can you help?

Can you help somebody with a cooked meal or raashan?

  1. Enter your details
    • Please mention your name or organisation name
    • Please specify if you are an NGO, a group or an individual
    • Add your contact number
  2. Select the persons/families you want to help by ticking the checkbox. Press 'Confirm the contribution' button, it will take you to the final screen.
  3. We suggest noting down the details (name, address, phone number, and request) or take a screenshot.
  4. Please enter the OTP sent on your number to verify and give the final confirmation. You can also check the details of the request in the completed list.
